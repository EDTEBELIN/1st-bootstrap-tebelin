Rounded Corner

The purpose of using rounded edges on images is often for aesthetic reasons, as it can make an image look softer and more approachable.

Additionally, rounded edges can be used to create a sense of continuity between different elements in a visual design, such as when all the images on a website or app have rounded edges to match the overall theme.

Rounded Circle

Round images can be used for aesthetic purposes, such as creating a more visually pleasing design, or for functional purposes, such as displaying a user's profile picture on a website or app.

The use of circular images has become increasingly popular in recent years, particularly in the realm of user interfaces and web design.

Thumbnail

Thumbnails are often used in digital media to provide a preview or summary of the contents of a file, such as a photograph or video clip.

Typically displayed in a grid or list format and can help users quickly navigate through large collections of visual content. They are also commonly used as clickable links that take users to the full-size version of the image or video.
